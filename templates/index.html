Special Courses

Special courses' concept is an model of education for person with disability. Owing to lack of knowledge, educational access and technology , disabled children often get's the proper resources which they actually need. So for them we here at CLYDE offers different courses for them like "SIGN LANGUAGE","BRAILLE SCRIPT" ,"LIPREADING" ,"BILINGUAL METHOD" and many more. According to the National Center for Education Statistics, about 11% of undergrads throughout the country report a disability. Online students with disabilities deserve equal access to the same resources, materials, and coursework as their classmates without disabilities. Working closely with Disability Services professionals, students are provided with the help and assistance they need to complete challenging coursework, be it: assistive technologies, readers, notetakers, interpreters, enlarged print, extra time for assignments and exams, and more! .Students can learn from these experts either in online as well as in offline mode according to their comfort. We hope at teaching them some helpful courses so that life may become a little easy for them.
